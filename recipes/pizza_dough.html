This is a barebones pizza dough recipe. The flour amounts are a little light to allow for adjustment later on in the process of the bread making. This means the recipe is relatively amenable to different types of flour (high gluten/whole wheat/all purpose)

  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 cup water (lukewarm)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cups flour
  1. Combine sugar, yeast, lukewarm water in a bowl and mix until dissolved (or mostly dissolved).
  2. Wait ~15 minutes for foam to appear (indicating the yeast is activated). This step is not required for rapid rise yeasts.
  3. Add flour and combine
  4. Adjust until dough is "right"
  5. Kneed for ~5-10 minutes
  6. Rise for ~50 minutes - 1.5 hours (depending on heat/humidity). Has the dough doubled in size?
  7. Punch down and form into a pizza dough.
  8. Bake pizza at 425Â°F
